AI systems have the power to transform industries and enhance lives, but they also pose significant challenges. From biased algorithms that perpetuate social inequities to opaque decision-making processes that obscure accountability, the risks are substantial. The MIRAI Toolbox is designed to help you:

  • Identify Bias: learn to detect and understand biases in AI models and datasets.
  • Assess Risk: evaluate the potential risks associated with deploying AI systems in various contexts.
  • Increase Transparency: gain insights into methods for making AI processes more transparent and explainable.
